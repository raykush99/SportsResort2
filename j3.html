Sales Representative



The Sales Representative's main job is to give customers outstanding customer service. This includes taking calls, replying to emails and live chats, and promptly and expertly handling customer complaints. The Sales Representative also has to advertise our company to potential customers and is in charge of the marketing department.

  • Sales representatives need to have critical thinking skills.
  • Sales representatives need to have good presentation skills.
  • Sales representatives need to have time management and organizational skills.
