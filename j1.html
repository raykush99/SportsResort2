Inventory Manager



In order to ensure effective supply chain management, the inventory manager is in charge of creating and using inventory control procedures, teaching other teammates about inventory management systems, and working closely with other departments. The inventory manager also manages any incoming shipments and insure that everything is in stock.

Pay

$55,000-$75,000 a year

Job type

Full-time

Shift and schedule

  • 8 hour shifts
  • No Nights
  • Monday to Friday

Location

Hargrove Rd SE, Smyrna, GA 30080

Added Benefits

  • Early access to new products
  • Employee discounts
  • Supplier relationships
  • Advanced analytics tools
  • Health insurance

Responsibilities

  • Overseeing inventory levels
  • Ensuring milestones and goals are met
  • Ensuring adherence to approved budget
  • Receiving, handling, storing, shipping, packaging, and transporting products
  • Locating items that may have incorrect locations or stock numbers
  • Communicating with the purchasing department
  • Communicating with the sales department
  • Evaluating inventory

Requirements

  • Proven experience in warehouse management or a similar role
  • Strong knowledge of warehouse operations, including order fulfillment and inventory control
  • Proficiency in using warehouse management systems to track inventory and manage workflows
  • Excellent leadership skills with the ability to effectively manage a team and drive performance
  • Effective communication skills to collaborate with cross-functional teams.
  • Ability to adapt to changing priorities in a fast-paced environment

Work Location
